Invisible

Game Introduction

Invisible is a sngle-player puzzle game. Players can explore the high school at night to find clues and resources.

Ideation of Invisible

  • The background story of Invisible
    The protagonist's childhood playmate jumped to his death at the school, but the teachers and classmates of are evasive about the details. In order to investigate the truth, the protagonist went into the campus at night to look for clues, but encounters an unseen presence...
  • The gameplay
    The player use limited resources and environmental elements to find and hide from invisible enemies
  • Core elements
    Puzzles + Survival + Fear
